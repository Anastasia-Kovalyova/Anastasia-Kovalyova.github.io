About Our Company

There are so many different sites that help you plan your wedding. But very few of them really help you achieve the desired result. The creator of our company all her life dreamed of helping a girl in creating the perfect wedding. And her goal was to make all preparations as convenient as possible. So she began to create a place in which there would be all the stylists, confectioners, photographers and the salon of wedding dresses and suits. We in Mon Amour treat each of our customers with great care and attention because each client is very important to us.
